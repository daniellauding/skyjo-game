üéØ Objective

Be the player with the lowest score after several rounds. The game ends when someone reaches 100+ points!

üéÆ Setup

  • Each player gets 12 cards in a 4√ó3 grid, face down
  • Flip over any 2 cards in your grid to start
  • The player with the highest sum of flipped cards goes first

üîÑ Your Turn

Choose one option:

  1. Draw from deck: Look at the card, then either:
    • Replace a card in your grid with it, OR
    • Discard it and flip a face-down card in your grid
  2. Take discard pile: Must replace a card in your grid with it

‚ö° Special Rule: Column Bonus

If you get 3 identical cards in a column, discard the entire column immediately! This removes those cards from your score.

üèÅ Round End

A round ends when a player reveals all 12 cards. Add up your card values - lower is better!

üéØ Card Values

üìä Numbers: Face value (-2 to 12) üö´ Higher cards = More points

üí° Strategy Tips

  • Remember what cards other players have revealed
  • Try to complete columns with identical cards
  • Keep track of low-value cards (-2, -1, 0, 1, 2)
  • End the round when you have a good score!
